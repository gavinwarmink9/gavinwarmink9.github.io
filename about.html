About Us

Giving Lethbridge Shelter Pets a Second Chance

Our Mission

SouthPaws Rescue partners with local shelters in Lethbridge to help dogs and cats find safe, loving homes. Our goal is to give every shelter pet the visibility and opportunity they deserve to be adopted.

We believe that every pet has a story, and sometimes all they need is a little help to reach their next chapter. By showcasing the amazing animals waiting in our local shelters, we hope to connect them with the perfect families right here in southern Alberta.
